About Us

This page is designed to learn the technical names of meat cuts.

Every animal's body is divided into primal cuts, which are the largest sections taken directly from the carcass. These are further subdivided into sub-primal cuts and retail cuts that you typically find in stores or restaurants. For example, in beef, the primal cuts are chuck, rib, loin, and round.
